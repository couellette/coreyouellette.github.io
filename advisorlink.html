    • This application provides a versatile innovative way for Financial Advisors to engage their clients, and receive actionable plans all real time. Advisors are no longer bound by paper, spreadsheets and desktop applications - AdvisorLink provided a medium to empower them no matter what device, or location may be in front of them.
Rethinking the way of financial engagement

Traditionally Financial Advisors would have to rely on less innovative, or much more expensive ways to get this level of data about their clients. This application needed to not only display this information in an easy to use responsive dashboard, but also needed to collect it. AdvisorLink is an all-in-one engagement platform that provided Advisors a on-stop solution for their client engagement needs.

Designing the application

We wanted to have an application that was information heavy, but still came across intuitive and easy to use. We streamlined the information without oversimplifying the data to create a truly holistic experience. The navigation is logical, the features are straight-forward, and once your account is activated you are ready to go.

Long-term gains

AdvisorLink was a unique experience that allowed me to apply my skills in a new industry. Being able to be the lead for User-Interface and Front-end development I had a strong level of autonomy in letting my creative ideas come into action.

My key responsibilities for this were:

    • Wireframing the UI of the application
