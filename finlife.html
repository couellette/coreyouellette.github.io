    • This application is an easy-to-use wealth management platform for financial advisors. It's purpose is aimed to help Financial Advisors educate and collaborate with their clients in a visual manner in order to give their clients a holistic financial approach to reaching their financial goals.
Realtime engagement of wealth managment

Other competing platforms provide a variety of ways for Financial Advisors to assist in the wealth management of their clients but are typically manual, or an overall dated process. Their approach was to provide transparent current information with proactive actionable suggestions to Financial Advisors and clients to assist in making optimal informed financial decisions for their overall financial future, values and goals. I was tasked with creating a framework that brought this process into the 21st century and created a experience that got Financial Advisors enthused to want to use an application such as this.

Designing the application

My marching orders were to create an application that made current market alternatives feel dated and less robust to what my clients' application had to offer. I was provided an application architecture but free reign on how to make the overall experience to the end user. I wanted to go with a colour scheme that was soft yet modern, and more importantly not make it look as if it was emulating a current application, or other financial institutions.
