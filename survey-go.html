Creating the Experience

Our goal was to provide a seamless and accessible survey-building experience that caters to both individuals and businesses. To achieve this, we designed the interface with familiarity in mind, incorporating user-friendly software elements that users encounter in their daily lives. We also prioritized speed and enjoyment, ensuring that the end product delivered an unparalleled user experience that exceeded our expectations.

Designing the website

We wanted to have a website that was modern and easy to use. We streamlined the information to create an enjoyable experience that cut to the point. An average user would only require a couple of minutes to get up and running with Survey-Go. The navigation is logical, the plans are straight-forward, and once you choose your account you are ready to go.

Long-term gains

This would fall under one of the larger undertaking done in my career. From start to finish Survey-Go took just under a year and I was the lead UX/UI and front-end developer for this whole undertaking.

My key responsibilities for this were:

    • Wireframing the UI of the website and survey tool application
    • Logo and visual creating of entire website
    • Usability testing of survey application
    • Front-end development of website, and survey application
    • Quality assurance of application
